Art Gallery

All works here are made by yours truly! Those made digitally are done in Adobe Fresco, a personal preference of mine despite having access to Adobe Photoshop through my university. Simpler is better! I tend to use one brush (the pencil brush) and few layers. Old habits die hard coming from traditional art. Other mediums I use include acrylic painting, oil pastels, mixed materials, Minecraft (it counts!), and pencil.

If you have any questions about my art, would like to send a comment, or are wondering about commissions, reach me at my email.
